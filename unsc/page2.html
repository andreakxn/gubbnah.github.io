HISTORY OF THE UNSC

A brief dive into our world

UNSC (EST. 2008)

Initially when the UNSC was created, it was on a player-friend basis. A user would friend an account to join our 'clan'. This was because the group feature had not yet existed.

UNSC HELLJUMPERS (EST. 2009)

Upon release of the groups feature, an official UNSC Helljumpers group created. This paved the path to the UNSC we know today.

The UNSC (EST. 2010)

Upon realisation that the Halo franchise on Roblox could be expanded on a much larger scale, the UNSC was created by VMKCillen.

1st UNSC Civil War (2010)

Led by Wii4, the UNSC Navy and ONI, a coup was launch to remove then-leader VMKCillen, and the Marine Corps from command. This proved successful, and a massive power struggle ensued.

The UNSC (Est. 2010)

In a counter towards the leadership of Wii4, Fondbeef and AustinPwnz with the assistance of ONI launched a new UNSC in attempt to seize power from Wii4. This was primarily due to Wii's attempt to shift the UNSC towards a Stargate-esque genre.

Wii's UNSC capitulates (2010)

Under pressure, the original UNSC group had capitulated. This resulted in a spring of UNSCs being created. 

UNSC-DF (EST. 2011)

In an attempt to reclaim the genre, VMKCillen with the assistance of Kanealii launched the UNSC-DF. With the recent release of Halo: Reach, this was a major success.

UNSC-DF capitulates (2011)

After a power struggle between Kanealii and VMKCillen, the UNSC-DF capitulated. The UNSC established by Fondbeef continued on.

2nd UNSC Civil War (2011-2012)

After Kanealii and VMKCillen joined forces with Keyrut, a massive power struggle ensued. VMKCillen launched a massive operation in attempt to coup Keyrut from power. This ultimately failed.

United Nations (EST. 2010, imp. 2012)

In a huge shift for the UNSC, a United Nations was established. This was primarily done to introduce a diplomacy / policy making aspect to the group. This was the beginning of the Mobile Bloxxers - UNSC alliance.

UWF/MB Coup attempt (2013)

After a formal dissolution of the United Nations in favour of the United World Federation, newly appointed UWF leader Horatio45 launched a coup attempt against the UNSC by convincing then-HIGHCOM to admin attack the UNSC in the name of MB. This was primarily done to weaken the UNSC's political power in the C&G community.

UWF-UNSC War (2013)

After the attack, the UNSC formally waged war against the UWF. This war is legally still an ongoing war and has never had a formal truce.

HISTORY OF THE UNSC (cont.)

It's a long wild ride. But you've made it this far, so we're sure you're interested.

AlphaLupi (EST. 2013)

In an attempt to once again regain control of the Halo community, VMKCillen made a comeback to the Halo genre under the alias of "AlphaLupi". There were several attempts prior to this, however, this proved the most successful - as it gained attention of the Roblox Staff. This later capitulated a couple months after establishment.

UNSC Renamed (2014)

During early 2014, after talks with Roblox, the UNSC was officially renamed to the original group name. This cemented the originality of the group.

UNSC-UWF War cont. (2014)

After an argument in regards to raid legitimacy, the UNSC formalised the continuation of hostilities against the UWF after multiple UWF raids occured. This resulted in an armistice.

UNSC Control Transferred (2015)

In an unprecedented move, Keyrut relinquished control of the UNSC to MathMinded. This ushered in many changes and reforms.

UNSC Relaunched (2018)

With the intent of launching for five weeks for a nostalgia event, the UNSC relaunched. This brought back hundreds of former members from UNSCs all over. This effectively destroyed the groups that currently dominated the genre.
