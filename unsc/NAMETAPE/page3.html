How do I wear it?

Follow the simple steps below!

  1. RIGHT CLICK AND SAVE - right click the image created. Give it any name you want. Remember where it is! You'll be uploading this soon.
  2. GO TO ROBLOX - once it's saved, click "Avatar" in your sidebar, press "Clothing" in the Avatar editor, and then "T-Shirts". Finally, press "Create" and upload your T-Shirt!
  3. WEAR IT - finally! Apply the T-Shirt to your character.
