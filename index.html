National Chinese Honor Society

of the Woodlands College Park High School

    Here at the Woodlands College Park High School's NCHS, we are committed to     bettering our community while sticking to our roots and fostering a diverse     group of students dedicated to learning more about Chinese history and     culture. It is our duty to set an example for all - both at school and abroad.

Catch us at a meeting!

    You can find us in Mrs. Lee's room or the classroom next door the first Monday     of every month at 3:15 p.m. or the following two Mondays for Chinese Club at     2:50 p.m.!

Inductees hold certificates of acceptance into NCHS
